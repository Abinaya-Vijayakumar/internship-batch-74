Overview

Wonderla Bangalore is situated on the Bangalore Mysore Road 28 KM from Bangalore city center and 120 KM from Mysore city. Wonderla Bangalore is ranked NO.1 amusement park and biggest in India. Wonderla Bangalore Wonderla incorporates the latest technologies including a number of water rides and latest imported land rides never seen elsewhere in the country. it has 60+ exhilarating rides such as Hurricane, Equinox, Insanity Twister, Recoil, Dropzone, Flash Tower, Mixer, Y-scream, Maverick etc. Wonderla Bangalore also has India's largest Indoor Musical Fountain & Laser Show apart from the Virtual Reality theatre that offers a complete multi-sensorial movie experience. this place is wonderful and Lovely entertainment place for families, kids and every age group of people. Families and kids can enjoy a number of water-based amusements that include two Wave Pools, two Play Pools, Lazy River and a variety of Water Slides. Wonder la is the only amusement park to provide heated water during the winters. Wonderla Bangalore meets the international levels of Safety & Hygiene standards an amusement park. Besides the wonderful rides, Wonderla also includes major attractions like musical fountains, rain showers, and reality shows. And while you are at the amusement park, you can never go hungry as the facility provides for five restaurants.

What's allowed

The appropriate outfits for our water rides are T-shirts, shorts, bermudas, three-fourths, swimsuits. The permitted clothing materials are made of 100% nylon or synthetic material.

What's not allowed

Clothes that put you at risk! The following types of outfits can put your health and safety at risk and are prohibited for water rides. Sarees, salwar, dupatta, formal pants, shirts, burkhas, shawls, scarves, school uniforms, jeans, three-fourths, cargoes, loose garments and anything other than the a forement list of permitted outfits.
